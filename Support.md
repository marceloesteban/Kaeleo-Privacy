# Kaeleo Support

For help with Kaeleo, privacy questions, deletion requests, or App Review
support, email [marceloluengoquinones@gmail.com](mailto:marceloluengoquinones@gmail.com).

In the app, open **Settings → About** for support and privacy links. Cloud AI
permissions can be reviewed or revoked at **Settings → Privacy Choices**.

Please do not include private notes, recordings, HealthKit data, or access
credentials in a support request unless support has specifically asked for a
sanitized diagnostic description.
